Technical Analysis Insights

A taste of what we teach inside the IJustWealth Bootcamp. Master these five fundamentals and you'll already be ahead of 90% of retail traders.

📊 Understand Market Structure First

Before any strategy, learn to identify higher highs, higher lows, lower highs and lower lows. Structure tells you the story of what price is doing — everything else is just confirmation.

🕯️ Candlesticks Are Communication

A candle doesn't just show price movement — it shows the battle between buyers and sellers. A long wick rejection at a key level tells you far more than any indicator ever will.

⚖️ Risk Management Is The Strategy

Protecting your capital is not a supplement to your strategy — it is the strategy. Risk 1–2% per trade maximum. Your job is to stay in the game long enough for your edge to play out.

🕐 Trade The Right Sessions

Not all hours are equal. The London open (08:00 UTC) and the London–New York overlap (13:00–17:00 UTC) produce the majority of clean, tradeable moves on XAUUSD and major Forex pairs.

📓 Journal Every Trade Without Exception

The traders who improve fastest are not the ones who read the most — they are the ones who review the most. Log every trade: entry, exit, reason, result, and how you felt.
